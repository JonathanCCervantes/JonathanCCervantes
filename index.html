Centralized Data Management

Simplify and streamline data collection, integrate and analysis complex ESG data. Saving time and reducing the need for extensive manual processes.

Pre-built ESG framework library

Leverage on our repository of information and align with international frameworks and corporate reporting standards that most fit ESG reporting approach for your organization such as SASB, TCFD and GRI.

ESG Materiality Assessment

Built-in tools for conducting ESG materiality assessments help your organization identify and prioritize the most relevant sustainability issues based on your potential impact on business performance and stakeholder interests.

Carbon Footprint Tracking

Advanced carbon accounting capabilities to allow your organization to measure and monitor your carbon footprint across the value chain, identifying areas for emissions reduction and carbon offsetting.

Supply Chain Transparency

Supply chain mapping tools to enable your organization to assess the sustainability performance of suppliers and identify risks and opportunities for improvement, promoting transparency and responsible sourcing practices.

Mitigation and Risk Control

Risk assessment modules help your organization to identify and mitigate ESG-related risks and opportunities, such as climate change, supply chain disruptions, and regulatory compliance issues, safeguarding business continuity and reputation.

Unified & Customizable Dashboard

User-friendly & customizable dashboards to provide a centralized view of all sustainability metrics, allowing you to track key performance indicators (KPIs) across environmental, social, and governance categories in real-time.

Generative AI Reporting

Flexible reporting capabilities enable you to generate analysis and narrative for a data-driven sustainability report to tailor specific needs, including reporting requirements and guidelines issued by SGX, compliance reports, stakeholder reports, and sustainability performance dashboards.
